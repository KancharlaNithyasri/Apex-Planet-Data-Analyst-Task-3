To optimize retail strategies for the Superstore, an RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis was conducted using Python to classify customer behavior.

## Key Structural Insights
1. **Champions Group:** This segment accounts for the highest share of total revenue.Strategic recommendation: Target them with exclusive loyalty programs and preview access to new product lines.
3. **At Risk Customers:** A significant group of previously high-frequency buyers haven't made a purchase in a long time.Strategic recommendation: Automated re-engagement email campaigns with specialized discount codes.
